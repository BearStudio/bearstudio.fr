A 30-minute exploration into the transition from architecture to UX design, highlighting how skills from architectural fields can powerfully enhance UX approaches. Emphasis is placed on the shared value of empathy and understanding user needs, crucial for crafting both physical spaces and digital interfaces where people feel at ease.
Through real-world examples and actionable tips, the presentation demonstrates how both architecture and UX design require a user-centered mindset and how inspiration can be drawn from diverse backgrounds.
