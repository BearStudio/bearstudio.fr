Delivery is one of the most important steps that should never be taken lightly when developing a mobile application. Just imagine the luxury of being able to run this process at any point in your development cycle, with (almost) no manual action and above all... being sure that there are no possible configuration errors, allowing you to ship a high-quality app!

After spending time trying to understand and test it, I present to you Fastlane, an amazing tool to achieve this, provided you know what you’re doing! I’ll walk you through all the details you need, along with the various nuances…
