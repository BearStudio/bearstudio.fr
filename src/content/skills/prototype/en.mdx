name: Prototype
description: Interactive mockups to test and validate user flows before development. Reduces risk, speeds up iteration, and ensures the product meets user needs.
icon: mobile-interaction
---

At BearStudio, prototyping is the step that follows wireframes. Where wireframes lay out the structure, the prototype goes further: it lets us validate user flows and iterate efficiently on usability, all within Figma.

The value is being able to navigate through the application before any code is written. Our clients can test screen sequences, react to interactions, and confirm that the behavior matches their expectations. Developers, on their side, start with a clear picture of what needs to be built. Everyone is aligned.

Iterating on a prototype is far faster than on code. A flow to rework, an interaction to adjust — a few hours in Figma is all it takes. It's this short feedback loop that lets us move fast and avoid surprises during development.
