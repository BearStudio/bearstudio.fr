name: Tailwind

icon: tailwind-css
---

At BearStudio, Tailwind CSS became a natural part of how we work. The idea is straightforward: instead of writing custom stylesheets for every element, we assemble small, reusable visual building blocks. It fits perfectly with our component-based approach, where each piece of the interface is self-contained and ready to be reused.

The most noticeable benefit is speed. Going from a mockup to a working component becomes seamless, testing a layout idea takes minutes, and back-and-forth with design teams is much shorter. On the performance side, the browser only loads what's actually used — no bloat, no extra scripts. The result simply loads fast.

Day to day, we rely on Tailwind for building our interface components as well as adapting colors, spacing, and visual identity to each client's project.
