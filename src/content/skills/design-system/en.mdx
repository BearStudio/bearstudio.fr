At BearStudio, a design system is both a shared visual language and a library of reusable components. The goal is simple: ensure interface consistency across the entire application, and speed up both design and development.

Our approach starts in Figma. That's where the design system takes shape: components, variants, colors, typography, spacing. Once validated on the design side, it's translated into code ready to use in the application. This bridge between design and development cuts out unnecessary back-and-forth and ensures that what's designed matches what's delivered.

In practice, we use Start UI as a starting foundation, which we then tailor to each project's needs. Whether it's a client who needs their own design system or one of our internal projects, the approach is the same: lay down solid foundations once, then build on top of them efficiently.
